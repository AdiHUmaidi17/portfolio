About Myself

My name is ADI HUMAIDI BIN ASMAN

I was born in 2003 at Kuala Lumpur Hospital. I live and grow up in Ampang, Selangor with my family members. I am the fifth child of five siblings.

I am a person who likes to challenge my weaknesses. This is because, for me, one of the biggest enemies for us is our own lack. By being able to fight against my shortcomings, I was able to achieve my goals.

I am also a person who likes to work in groups. In my opinion, if a problem can be solved by one person, surely the problem can be solved more quickly and effectively if it is solved in a group in the right way. This is because when working in groups, we will get solution ideas from various parties. The best ideas can be finalized through discussion and can be used to solve problems.
